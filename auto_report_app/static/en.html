What BaZi Reads

BaZi converts birth year, month, day, and hour into Four Pillars. It is not a simple “missing element” system; it reads how elements flow, how Ten Gods act, and how major luck and annual timing activate the chart.

Ming Atelier turns this into a practical life-script reading: structure first, then career, wealth, relationship rhythm, risk boundaries, and what to do next.

Four Pillars
Year shows background; month shows career field; day shows self and relationships; hour shows future extension.
Five Elements
Metal, Wood, Water, Fire, and Earth describe rhythm, resources, pressure, and action.
Ten Gods
They explain how you express, earn, learn, take responsibility, and relate to people.
Timing
The natal chart is the base map; major luck is climate; annual luck is activation.
